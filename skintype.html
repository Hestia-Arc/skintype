INTRODUCTION

Knowing about your skin type is important in your skincare journey. This helps you objectnot to misjudge and saves you lots of worries deciding the right products to use. It helps you understand your skin better, the kind of products that suits it, how to maintain and have a routine care for it.

Basically there are three (3) types of skin and each has different characteristics.

  1. Normal skin
  2. Oily skin
  3. Dry skin

Two other skin types are: - Combination skin and Sensitive skin.

Now let's dive into each skin type characteristics, identifying and how to maintain them.

NORMAL SKIN

Have you ever imagined having a smooth, gorgeous, super dope skin? Here it is, this skin type is quite easy to identify.

CHARACTERISTICS OF A NORMAL SKIN

  • Smooth texture
  • Hardly breaks out
  • No blemishes/ imperfections
  • No flakiness
  • No shiny patches
  • Unnoticeable pores

Usually there is no major skin issues. The skin is even and well balanced so it doesn't cause you much hassles.

SKINCARE TIPS FOR NORMAL SKIN

  • Use a gentle cleanser
  • A toner
  • A hydrating exfoliant
  • An antioxidant rich serum
  • A moisturizer
  • Sunscreen
  • You can make use of facial oils too.

OILY SKIN

People with this skin type facial oil glands produce excessive amount of sebum which clog the pores which makes them reach out for blotting sheets often. The cause of oily skin are mostly climate, hormonal change/hormonal imbalance.

CHARACTERISTICS OF OILY SKIN

  • Feel greasy and shiny all over.
  • Large pores that get easily clogged from sweat and oils the body secretes.
  • Frequently breaks out.
  • Less prone to wrinkles.

SKINCARE TIPS FOR OILY SKIN

  • Cleanse the face often.
  • Avoid oil-based cleanser, heavy creams or moisturizers to minimize the appearance of oil.
  • Needs less moisturizer throughout the day.
  • Use AHA and BHA pore clearing exfoliants.
  • Antiseptic botanicals to effectively reduce breakouts.
  • Stay hydrated.

DRY SKIN

This type of skin lacks lipids that is needed to retain moisture. The cause of it could be weather, low air humidity, sun exposure, hot showers and over exfoliating.

CHARACTERISTICS OF DRY SKIN

     It feels tight and rough

     Looks dull
 
     Uneven texture
	 
     Skin elasticity is low
 
     Flakiness
 
     Possible itchiness

SKUNCARE TIPS FOR DRY SKIN

     Keep it hydrated.

     Limit hot showers.

     Use a gentle cleanser.

     Make use of a cream moisturizer regularly.

     Dont forget your sunscreen.
